Try to Stay

Gameplay Guide

Brief

Redefine Fun with a Modern Board Game Featuring Unique Rules and Design!

How to Play

  • Your zone: Each player has their own zone, and the zone color matches the player's color.
  • Roll the Dice: Roll the dice to determine your moves on the board.
  • Earn Points: Land on an opponent’s colored zone to earn points from them.
  • Boost Your Zone: If you land on your zone, you won’t earn any points unless you boost your zone. Press the "Boost" button to choose an opponent’s color. Once boosted, your zone will have borders in the chosen opponent’s color, visually confirming the boost's success. You’ll then earn points in the chosen opponent’s color when landing on your boosted zone.
  • Avoid Purple Zones: There are two purple zones on the board. Landing on these will cost you points.

Winning the Game

  • Eliminate Opponents: Collect 5 points from a single opponent to eliminate them. The eliminated player will be marked as "Ejected" and cannot continue. Shown in the format below:
    • Ejected
  • Be the Last Player Standing: The game continues until only one player remains who hasn’t been eliminated. This player is the winner!

Special Features

  • Point Tracker: Your points are tracked with colors displayed in rows. Each row represents the points you’ve gained from landing on opponent zones or your boosted zone, shown in the format below:
  • Red Cards: Earn red cards by eliminating opponents. The player with the most red cards leads the leaderboard.
  • Movement Tracker: When it’s your turn, 6 potential zones are displayed on the board to show the possible zones you could land on after rolling the dice.
  • Reroll Dice: If you’re not satisfied with your dice roll, you can choose to reroll for a better outcome.
  • Chat and Compete: Enjoy real-time chat with other players while you strategize and compete.
