Try to Stay - Instructions

Overview

Try to Stay is a board game for 2-4 players. Roll the dice to move around the board and earn points. Each player’s zone matches their color but does not earn points until customized. To customize, press the "Customize" button and choose an opponent’s color. Once customized, your zone will earn points when landing on both your customized zone and the opponent’s zones. Avoid purple zones that cost points. The winner is the last player remaining who hasn’t been kicked out of the game.

How to Play

  • Roll the Dice: Move based on the dice roll. Use your moves strategically to earn points and avoid pitfalls.
  • Earn Points: Land on an opponent’s colored zone to earn points from them.
  • Customize Your Zone: Land on your own zone and press "Customize" to change its color to an opponent’s color. Your zone will then earn points when landing on both your customized zone and the opponent’s zones.
  • Avoid Purple Zones: There are two purple zones on the board. Landing on these will cost you points.

Winning the Game

  • Eliminate Opponents: Collect 5 points from a single opponent to eliminate them. The eliminated player will be marked as "Ejected" and cannot continue. Shown in the format below:

  • Be the Last Player Standing: The game continues until only one player remains who hasn’t been eliminated. This player is the winner!
